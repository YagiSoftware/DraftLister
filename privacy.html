Personal Information the App Collects

When you install the App, we are automatically able to access certain types of information from your Shopify account:

  1. Your shop name and shop contact email, this is only used for support and emergency contact purpose.


  2. Draft Orders data, including the draft order ID, draft order Invoice number, last updated date, total price and linked customer ID (unique string), this is only used for the app functionality.


  3. Unique Identifier (ID) of your customer, this is used to identiy each individual customer and show the corresponding draft orders to that customer, no personal identifiable information is being accessed. This data is requested from Shopify every time a customer views the draft order list page, this data is never stored in our app database.


Please refer to the Data Protection Agreement to see in detail what data is accessed, stored on the database, data retention period, these are strictly for app functionality purpose.

How Do We Use Your Personal Information?

We use the personal information we collect from you (your store name and contact email) and your draft orders and customers data in order to provide the Service and to operate the App, and to troubleshoot customer support issues if needed.
